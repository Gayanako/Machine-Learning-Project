How to Monitor Your Health at Home

Monitoring your health at home has become easier with the right devices. Here's how you can check your blood pressure and cholesterol levels:

Blood Pressure Monitor

A blood pressure monitor is essential for regularly tracking your blood pressure at home. There are two main types:

  • Automatic Arm Blood Pressure Monitors - These are the most accurate and easy to use. They are designed to inflate the cuff automatically.
  • Automatic Wrist Blood Pressure Monitors - These are more compact and portable but may not be as accurate as arm monitors.

Where to Buy: Blood pressure monitors are available on popular e-commerce sites or your local pharmacy.

Cholesterol Testing Kit

A cholesterol testing kit allows you to monitor your cholesterol levels at home. These kits typically require a small blood sample (fingerstick) and provide results in minutes.

Where to Buy: You can purchase cholesterol testing kits from pharmacies, health stores, or online retailers such as Amazon.
